About Author


Nevena Vidakovic, 29/20



My name is Nevena Vidakovic and I'm from Belgrade, Serbia. I've been into tattoos for a long time, and I've had love for drawing and painting since I was a kid. I've always been into different and weird things and I have passion for tattoing and grunge style. My dream for now is to keep up with practising and open my own studio one day.
